Some common questions
were often asked

How to open a patient account?

You have to go to sign up and then fill in your details to open your account. You will find another page to add more details about you after that.

How to open a doctor account?

You have to go to sign up and then click Register as Doctor. Then fill in your details to open your account. You will find another page to add more details about you after that.

How to login to my account?

You have to go to login and then type your credentials to login.

What should I do if I forget my password?

You should go to login and then click Reset password and enter your email there. You will get an email with a reset link.

How to get an appointment?

Go to doctors page and search for your desired doctor. Then go to their profile, you will get a button for appointment. Give the date of appointment you want to set.

How to see my appointments?

Login to your account then go to My Appointments to see all your appointments.
